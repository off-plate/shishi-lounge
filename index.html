A safe spot
to slow down

ShiShi Lounge started eight years ago as a small room with big pipes. Today it’s a candle-lit cellar where the city exhales — soft leather, warm brick and smoke that curls toward the vaulted ceiling.

Pick a flavour, pour a drink, grab a board game or a controller, and let the night take its time. No rush. No clock. Just you, the people you came with, and the slow burn of good coal.
